FCSC Redistricting

As Fayette County School Corporation works on redistricting, there has been a lot of data and maps produced. The data are largely unavailable to the public and the maps are locked in PDF files. This site is a citizen-led effort to grant access to the data behind these redistricting proposals to ALL citizens of Fayette County.

Efforts to contact FCPS about gaining access to the geographic data produced by Davis Demographics & Planning, Inc. have gone unanswered. However, by leveraging current school district boundary data from Lexington's Open Data Portal, PDF maps from FCPS's redistricting page, and FOSS mapping applicatons like QGIS and GeoJSON.io, we have attempted to recreate the data being used by FCPS.

**This is a work in progress. You can contribute through the GitHub repo.**
